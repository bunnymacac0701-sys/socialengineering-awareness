What is Scareware?

Scareware is malicious software or deceptive messaging designed to frighten users into believing their system is infected or compromised.

Common Tactics

  • Fake virus detection pop-ups
  • Urgent countdown timers
  • Requests for immediate payment
  • Fake tech support numbers

How to Stay Safe

  • Do not click suspicious pop-ups
  • Close browser via task manager if locked
  • Use legitimate antivirus software
  • Never call numbers from random pop-ups
